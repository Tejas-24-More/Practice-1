Data analysis is the process of inspecting, cleansing, transforming,
 and modeling data with the goal of discovering useful information,
  informing conclusions, and supporting decision-making.
   Data analysis has multiple facets and approaches, 
   encompassing diverse techniques under a variety of names, 
   and is used in different business, science, and social science domains.
    In today's business world, data analysis plays a role in making decisions more scientific 
    and helping businesses operate more effectively
